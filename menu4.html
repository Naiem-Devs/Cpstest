Click speed test

Cps test allows you to test your finger speed on mouse to define how speedily you can click on the mouse button. The faster you click the faster you can break the records.

How many clicks per second is fast?

To become the faster in clicks you need to click between 5 to 10 clicks per second. CpsTest Logo defined some ranks upon your clicking speed. But if you want to avoid becoming turtle then you must click above 5 clicks per second.
